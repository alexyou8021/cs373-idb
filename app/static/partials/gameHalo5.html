Halo 5: Guardians

Initially revealed at Microsoft's E3 2013 Media Briefing, Halo 5: Guardians is a sci-fi first-person shooter developed by 343 Industries and published by Microsoft Studios, set to release on October 27. 2015 on the Xbox One. The game is the 5th installment in the mainline Halo games. The game features heavily reworked audio and a new engine built for the Xbox One. The game's campaign and multiplayer will run at 60 frames per second. The game's soundtrack is composed by composer Kazuma Jinnouchi, who also worked on Halo 4's soundtrack. The game's creative director is Tim Longo, most notable for his work on the Star Wars games.
